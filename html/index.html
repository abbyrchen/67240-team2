Raceday is April 12-13

Buggy is a unique race that has been held on and behind the Carnegie Mellon University campus during the spring carnival since 1920. Teams of five athletes (the pushers) push an un-motorized vehicle (a buggy) containing a driver around a 0.84 mile course in a relay format where the vehicle serves as the baton.

The CMU Buggy Alumni Association is an Alumni Interest Group from Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA. Please contact admin@cmubuggy.org with any questions.
