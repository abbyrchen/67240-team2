The Buggy Alumni Association was founded in 2008 to unite and connect alumni across teams and eras, and to support the coolest sport we know. Join us! We're open to everyone: current students, Pittsburgh locals, enthusiasts of any kind.

Current Students, we have resources for you too!

One of the Buggy Alumni Association's primary goals is to act as a repository for Buggy history. We've worked hard to collect both historical records and stories of Sweepstakes through the years. We’ve also collected a repository of buggy resources for current students, including how to start a team, build a buggy, and make use of the BAA Endowment Fund! Find them under Buggy Reference.

Explore Buggy Team Resources >

Buggy is a unique race that has been held on and behind the Carnegie Mellon University campus during the spring carnival since 1920. Teams of five athletes (the pushers) push an un-motorized vehicle (a buggy) containing a driver around a 0.84 mile course in a relay format where the vehicle serves as the baton.
